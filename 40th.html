40th Anniversary

Below you will find an overview of the events planned for CSH's 40th Anniversary. This is a general overview and subject to change. More specifics will be announced as plans are finalized and the date of 40th approaches.

The Formal Banquet will be held on Saturday, April 16 at 7:00 PM in the Davis Room of the Student Alumni Union.

Event Calendar

  • Friday, April 15
  • Saturday, April 16
  • Sunday, April 17

Capture the Disk

Starting around dusk we'll be playing Capture the Disk. Like the name suggests, Capture the Disk is a game similar to Capture the Flag, but with a CSH spin. Rules will be explained prior to the start of the game. There will be an info session to teach new players and help old players learn the rule changes that have occurred over the years. The session will be at 7pm in NRH 1250.

Plates

After Capture the Disk is finished it's time to get plates! Small groups will organize and go to various restaurants. Whether you like Mark's, Nick Tahou's, Steve T, or another place entirely this is the time to undo any kind of remotely healthy exercise Capture the Disk provided.

Messed Up Movie Night (FUMN)

Messed Up Movie Night (FUMN) is a gathering of like minded individuals looking for some good, ol' fashioned depraved, disturbing, or sometimes just plain awful, film. A few times a year The Committee descends upon house to screen approximately 10 hours of both feature and short films in one sitting. Some attendees have a skewed view of what the words "Messed Up" means... Messed Up is somewhat subjective... This subjectivity is best illustrated by the diversity of the list below as well as the reactions of the attendees themselves... Often arguments will break out after a particular film is screened between those who thought the film was Messed Up and those who think The Committee has lost it's mind.

Tours of Gene Polisseni Center

Saturday afternoon you can tour the new RIT hockey arena. Tours for this event will be in smaller groups. The tour will begin at 2PM and interested members are asked to gather in the lobby of the Polisseni Center at this time.

Tour Floor

Haven't been to floor in years? Want to come hang out? Tours of floor and probably many stories from past and present generations will occur all afternoon.

Formal Banquet

In the evening there will be a formal banquet held in the Davis Room of the Student Alumni Union at 7:00 PM Saturday. There will be a buffet of Italian food including Chicken Piccata, Tortellini, Salads, and a dessert of Tiramisu! There will be plenty of refreshments, a dance floor, and a brief speech on the current state of Computer Science House.

Goodbye Brunch

From 10am to 12pm in the Davis Room, brunch is one last get together before everyone heads out. T-Shirts designed specially for 40th will be given out at this event.

Hotel Reservations

CSH has reserved a block of rooms at the Hampton Inn & Suites Rochester/Henrietta for the event. The hotel has created a reservation portal for members to reserve rooms in our hotel block at a special rate. Rooms start at $99/night until March 15th or until the block is sold-out, whichever comes first.

Childcare

April 05, 2016 We've received a lot of questions recently regarding child care during the formal dinner portion of 40th. We are sad to announce we will be unable to provide child care or recommend a location for use. If there are Rochester local parents who can recommend a babysitting service or local daycare for use to other parents, we will be happy to add links or phone numbers to the 40th website.

We apologize for getting this information to all of you late, as we had been hoping we still may be able to locate something. This will be reflected in the post-mortem notes as something to figure out much earlier for 45th (and beyond), as well as to work with actual CSH alumni parents who have experience with places nearby. If you have any concerns or questions feel free to email the Chairman.

Parking

April 04, 2016 There is actually no ticketing or towing on campus (for non flagrant parking, so if you park in a handicapped spot or in a fire lane I make no promises). C-Lot, B-Lot and even the "20-minute spots" outside NRH are all fair game both Saturday and Sunday (as well as Friday after 5pm). If you arrive before 5pm on Friday you can get a free 24 hour parking pass for dorm lots (aka C-Lot). See the RIT Parking website for more info on that.
